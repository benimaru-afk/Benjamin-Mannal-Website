  • Motorcycle Renting System

    Description: Discover the Wheels Bicycle Hire System, an innovative and efficient solution designed to revolutionize the way a local bicycle hire shop operates. Developed as a group project, this system enhances the customer experience and streamlines shop management by automating and organizing all aspects of bike rentals. All code written in Java. Key Features: Comprehensive Bike Database: Easily manage and access details for each bike, including type, size, make, model, daily charge rate, and deposit. Customer Records: Maintain detailed records of all customers and their hire history for seamless repeat business. Automatic Cost Calculation: Quickly determine hire costs based on the number of days a bike is rented. Efficient Transaction Management: Record all hire transactions, including start date, duration, customer details, and bike information, for easy retrieval. Unified Receipts: Generate a single receipt for customers hiring multiple bikes, even if rented for different durations. Automated Return Processing: Calculate actual hire duration and any additional charges upon bike return. Payment Tracking: Keep a clear record of the total amount due and payments made by each customer. Receipt Printing: Print professional receipts for every transaction. Bike Status Tracking: Monitor whether bikes are in stock, hired out, or under repair. Specialist Bike Details: Record additional information for specialized bikes to cater to all customer needs. Experience the future of bicycle hire management with the Wheels Bicycle Hire System, where convenience meets innovation!

  • Inn Reservation Management System

    Description: Introducing the Inn Reservation Management System, a sophisticated solution tailored to streamline operations at Inn, a charming new hotel. Designed for seamless integration and user-friendliness, this system ensures efficient reservation management and enhances the overall guest experience. All code written in Java. Key Features: Guest Registration: Effortlessly register guests through phone calls or walk-ins, with data entry handled by the receptionist. Room Booking: Easily book rooms by specifying check-in and check-out dates, as well as the number of adults and children. Guests can choose from various room classes (Deluxe, Standard, Premium, Presidential). Room Status Management: Keep track of room statuses (Occupied, Ready to Clean, Available) with real-time updates. Cleaning Coordination: Service staff can view rooms ready to clean and update their status to available once ready. Receptionist Functions: Book and check out rooms, update room statuses, and manage guest information with ease. Owner Insights: Owners can view room statuses daily and generate summary reports on reservations for a week, month, or year. Secure Access: All staff and owners log in with a unique username, password, and role, ensuring secure access to the system. Comprehensive Room Information: Maintain detailed room information, including floor number, status, room number, class, and price. Guest Details Management: Keep thorough records of guest information, including unique identifiers, names, contact details, and booking history. System Components: Guest Information: Unique ID, first name, last name, email address, phone number. Room Information: Unique ID, floor number, status, room number, class, price. Dynamic Combat System: Engage in fast-paced, strategic combat with a variety of enemy types, each with unique abilities and attack patterns. Increasing Difficulty: Face progressively challenging levels, each designed to test your skills and strategic thinking. System Components: Character Profiles: Choose between Bard and Knight, each with unique skill sets, weapons, and abilities.
